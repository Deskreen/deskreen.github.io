Advanced Screen Sharing Tips

Take your Deskreen experience to the next level with these advanced tips and tricks.

Optimizing Performance

For the best screen sharing experience, consider these performance optimization tips:

Network Configuration

  • Use a wired connection when possible for maximum stability
  • Ensure your router supports modern Wi-Fi standards (Wi-Fi 6 recommended)
  • Position devices close to your router to minimize latency

Display Settings

  • Adjust resolution based on your secondary device capabilities
  • Use lower frame rates for static content to save bandwidth
  • Enable hardware acceleration when available

Security Best Practices

  • Always use the latest version of Deskreen
  • Regularly update your network passwords
  • Monitor connected devices in the Deskreen dashboard
  • Disconnect unused sessions promptly

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Connection Problems

If you're experiencing connection issues:

  1. Check your firewall settings
  2. Verify both devices are on the same network
  3. Restart the Deskreen application
  4. Clear your browser cache on the receiving device

Performance Issues

For slow or laggy performance:

  • Reduce the screen resolution
  • Close unnecessary applications
  • Check your network bandwidth
  • Update your graphics drivers

These tips will help you get the most out of your Deskreen setup!
